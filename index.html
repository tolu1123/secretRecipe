THE IMMORTAL CHICKEN

Have you ever wanted to prepare a tantalizing dish for your Husband or your Children? Or you are a student who would like to celebrate his or her day alone behind closed doors. Do not worry any longer, Chef Tolu is here to guide you on how to go on the journey of preparing this Chicken Immortal (aka Chicken Peri Peri).

LEVEL

Begineers

TIME

50mins

RATINGS

INGREDIENTS (5 Adult servings)
Chicken Laps/Thigh 15pcs
Vegetable-Oil 1 bottle
Garlic 5 cloves
Onions 3 medium bulbs
Curry 2 satchets
Thyme 2 satchets
Dry Pepper 10 Table spoons
Seasoning 8 cubes
NUTRITION (per Chicken Lap)
KCAL 240kcal
FAT 7g
SATURATES 4g
PROTEIN 21g
FIBRE 3g
SALT 0.2g

HOW TO PREPARE
CHICKEN IMMORTAL

  • STEP 1

    Take the Chicken laps/thighs and cut the undesirable parts(The Chicken feet) away. Then rinse the Chicken laps in water.

  • STEP 2

    Put a pot on a lighted stove with about 2 litres of water. Then put the rinsed Chicken laps in the pot. Then add sliced garlic, sliced onions, seasoning, salt, thyme and curry in the pot. Allow the contents of the pot to boil for about 25minutes.

  • STEP 3

    Once the cooking of the Chicken is done, put a large frying on the lighted stove, then pour the Vegetable-Oil in the frying pan and allow to heat up. Once the Oil is heated up, carefully put the Chicken laps in the heated oil to prevent spilling. Then allow to fry in the Oil till the coat of the Chicken meat becomes golden brown.

  • STEP 4

    Once the frying is done. Spread the Suya pepper in a large bowl, slice some onions, mix both the pepper and the onions in the bowl. After you have done this, sprinkle the mixed pepper and onions on the meat till it becomes coated.

  • STEP 5

    You can now serve the CHICKEN IMMORTAL with any drink of your choice.

  • INFO FOR INNER CIRCLE MEMBERS

    Once you are done with the meal. Remember to close all the windows to prevent word from getting to your neigbours that you are about to take a meal that makes you experience the feeling of mortal immortality.
